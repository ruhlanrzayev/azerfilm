Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to use this website?
Yes, all features like browsing, rating, reviewing, and watching trailers are completely free.
How often are new films and series added?
New titles are added every week! We also add upcoming trailers, so you never miss the latest releases.
Is the information about movies, series, and actors verified?
Yes, we collect data from trusted sources and update them regularly to keep everything correct and fresh.
Can I edit or delete my review after posting it?
Of course! Go to your profile, find your review, and you will see options to edit or delete it anytime.
Where do the movie and series trailers come from?
All trailers are official, provided by production studios or authorized distributors.
Do I need an account to leave a review?
Yes, you need to create a free account to leave ratings and reviews. It helps us keep the community safe and fair.
How can I rate a movie or series?
You can rate any movie, series, actor, or director by clicking the "Rate" button on their page. Just choose your stars and share your thoughts!
